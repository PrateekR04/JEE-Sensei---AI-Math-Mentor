TOPIC: Important Calculus Formulas (JEE Level)

Limits:
lim x→0 sinx/x = 1
lim x→0 (e^x - 1)/x = 1
lim x→0 (1 + x)^(1/x) = e

Derivatives:
d/dx (x^n) = nx^(n-1)
d/dx (sinx) = cosx
d/dx (cosx) = -sinx
d/dx (e^x) = e^x
d/dx (lnx) = 1/x

Product rule:
d(uv)/dx = u'v + uv'

Quotient rule:
d(u/v)/dx = (v u' - u v') / v²

Chain rule:
d(f(g(x)))/dx = f'(g(x)) * g'(x)

Second derivative:
d²y/dx² = derivative of dy/dx
